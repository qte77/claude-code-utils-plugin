# Validation Criteria

Quality thresholds for each pipeline phase. Used by validation loops to determine
whether a phase output passes, needs revision, or should escalate.

## Phase 0 — Analyzing Source Project

- [ ] Technical capability summary covers: architecture, tech stack, scalability signals
- [ ] At least 3 differentiating technical capabilities identified
- [ ] Limitations and risks section present
- [ ] Sources: all claims backed by code/docs evidence

## Phase 1A — Researching Industry Landscape

- [ ] Minimum 5 direct or adjacent competitors analyzed
- [ ] Each competitor entry includes: positioning, pricing tier, target segment, strengths/weaknesses
- [ ] Market map identifies whitespace opportunities
- [ ] Sources: public data only, URLs cited

## Phase 1B — Researching Market

- [ ] TAM/SAM/SOM estimates with methodology stated
- [ ] Growth rate cited with source and year
- [ ] Buyer persona defined: role, pain, budget authority
- [ ] Integrates findings from Phase 0 and Phase 1A

## Phase 2 — Validating Product-Market Fit

- [ ] PMF score produced (0-10) with rationale per dimension
- [ ] Evidence for and against PMF both present
- [ ] At least 2 PMF risks identified with mitigation suggestions

## Phase 3 — Developing GTM Strategy

- [ ] Customer segmentation: at least 2 segments with ICP definition
- [ ] Channel recommendations ranked by reach/cost/fit
- [ ] 90-day launch plan with milestones

## Phase 4 — Analyzing Contradictions

- [ ] At least 3 cross-phase tensions or contradictions surfaced
- [ ] Each contradiction has a recommended resolution
- [ ] Objection list for top 5 sales/investor objections with responses

## Phase 5 — Synthesizing Research

- [ ] All prior phases referenced with key findings summarized
- [ ] Single integrated narrative connects source → market → GTM
- [ ] Confidence rating per major claim (High/Medium/Low)

## Phase 6 — Generating Slide Deck

- [ ] Deck structure follows config/comments_gtm.md audience preference
- [ ] Every slide has a clear headline (insight, not topic)
- [ ] Data slides cite source inline
- [ ] No slide exceeds 5 bullet points

## Escalation Threshold

If any mandatory criterion (marked with [ ]) fails after max retries (see validation-loops.md),
escalate to human review before proceeding to the next phase.
